Ai WeiWei

Artist and activist, Ai Weiwei explores social and politicial issues with a diversity of mediums from gunpowder and pyrotecnics to ink and sculpture. He is a modern artist who has truly shaped and defined the limitless potential for exploration of medium. Internationally known, Weiwei works in New York city.
